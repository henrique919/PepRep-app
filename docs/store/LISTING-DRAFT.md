## Full description (draft)

PepRep is a measurement instrument for peptide reconstitution arithmetic and personal
record-keeping.

You enter every number — vial amount, diluent volume, and the dose you intend to draw.
PepRep shows the working (concentration, volume, and units on a U-100 syringe), warns about
overflow and under-draw, and keeps an auditable history of what you actually logged.

**What PepRep is**
- Exact reconstitution math from your inputs
- Optional vial inventory derived from your log (append-only ledger)
- Plans and local reminders you define
- Plaintext export and optional password-protected encrypted backups you control

**What PepRep is not**
- Not medical advice
- Does not recommend doses, schedules, stacks, or protocols
- No account and no cloud sync in v1
- No analytics or advertising

A U-100 syringe is always 100 units per millilitre. Syringe “30 / 50 / 100” sizes are barrel
volume only (for overflow warnings and the gauge), never a units-per-mL multiplier.

PepRep is a measurement tool. It is not medical advice and does not recommend doses. You are
responsible for checking every result against your vial and syringe.
